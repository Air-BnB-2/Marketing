Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How much does the software cost?

A. The software is free of cost!

Q. How does the software work?

A. The software returns the price based on a machine learning model driven by previous market data.

Q. How long does it take to get an estimate?

A. The software should return useful information in less than 3 minutes.

Q. Is there a feature for niche categories such as luxury attractions?

A. Yes. Options such as high speed wifi, entertainment services, and luxury attractions all exist.

Q. Does the model include options outside of the United States?

A. No. Market data volatility and international regulations don't allow us to extend the model outside of the United States.
